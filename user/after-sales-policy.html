After-Sales Policy
Policy updated on 2026-07-01
Return Policy

7-Day No-Questions-Asked Return

Within 7 calendar days of receipt, if the product is intact and resalable, you may apply for a no-questions-asked return.

Return Conditions

  • Product is unused, unwashed, and unopened (for sealed products)
  • Original packaging, tags, manuals, free gifts, and other accessories are complete
  • Retain the original purchase receipt or electronic order record

Not Eligible Cases

  • Exceeds the 7-day return window
  • Product has been used or washed
Refund Policy

Refund Timeline

Stripe Payment: Refunds will be returned to the original payment method within 3-5 business days after approval.

PayPal Payment: Refunds will be returned to the original payment method within 1-3 business days after approval.

Refund Method

All refunds are returned via the original payment method. Changing the refund channel is not supported. The actual arrival time depends on the processing speed of each payment institution.

Price Protection

If the product price drops within 7 calendar days after purchase, you may apply for price protection to receive a refund of the price difference. The difference will be returned to the original payment account, with the same refund timeline as above.

Exchange Policy

Same Product, Different Variant Exchange

Within 7 calendar days of receipt, you may apply to exchange for the same product in a different color or size. The product must be intact, unused, and with complete original packaging.

Exchange Process

1

Submit an exchange request on the order details page and select the desired variant

2

After customer service approval, send back the original product

3

After the warehouse receives and confirms the product is intact, the new variant will be shipped

Shipping Fee Policy

Merchant Responsibility

For returns or exchanges due to merchant reasons such as product quality issues or incorrect shipments, both-way shipping fees are fully covered by MOYUYO.

Customer Responsibility

For returns or exchanges due to non-merchant reasons such as personal preference or incorrect size, the return shipping fee is borne by the customer. For exchanges, the shipping fee for the new product is covered by the merchant.

Shipping Insurance

Some products come with shipping insurance, which can be used to offset return shipping fees. Please refer to the product detail page and insurance company rules for specifics.

Non-Returnable & Non-Exchangeable Items
Grooming products that have been washed, used, or opened
Customized products, including engraved/embroidered personalized items
Intimate or hygiene products such as underwear, pet beds (after opening)
Consumable products such as food, treats, and supplements (after opening)
Promotional free gifts, samples, and other non-sale items
Products beyond the return/exchange window
